What did you Just F***ing Do today?

August 21, 2013Determination, Inspiration, MotivationDetermination, Inspiration, Motivationjfdikarla

As you’ll know from my last post, I love lists. But sometimes I have lists which contain boring ‘house jobs’, which I procrastinate over. There’s always something more exciting to do. In order to motivate myself, I try to tap into that feeling of satisfaction I know I’ll get once the job’s done. And then the other morning I woke up to an email from my Mum. It made me smile from ear to ear as she’d embraced JFDI and got on with one of her niggling house jobs. Just had to share it…

Dearest You,

I thought you’d like to know how much you have inspired me!! “Just F***ing Do It” has become my mantra. At the weekend I faced the horrid job of painting the garage door and I so did not want to do it! But I kept telling myself “Just F***ing Do It” as no-one else was going to. So I forced myself to go and do it. But, while painting the door, I found that the white surrounding bits were also in need of painting. And that required white gloss paint and I so did not want to do that either! But again, I kept telling myself “Just F***ing Do It” and guess what…

today I “just f***ing did it”  !!!!!

Afterwards I was tired, dirty and stank of turps from cleaning my hands and brushes. I’d left a mess in the kitchen and of course there was no cup of coffee or meal prepared for me. But I kept thinking “I don’t care, I’ve done it!!”

I just love it now! When I go out through the side gate, I turn to look and admire my work as I pass!!!

Nice one Mum!
