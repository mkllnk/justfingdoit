Those troublesome weeds

December 18, 2013Dealing with issues, Mental healthDealing with issues, Mental Healthjfdikarla

I decided to weed the garden today. By garden, I mean our small back yard which has a fairly large rectangular section covered by little red stones. It’s not the most attractive feature. I can only imagine the landlord wanted a quick fix to tidy up the unpaved area, perhaps in a hurry to rent the property.
